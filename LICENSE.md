**Solutions:**
1. Run `StandLoader.exe` as Administrator

### 🟡 Menu Doesn't Appear After Injection

**Symptoms:** Injection reports success but the overlay is invisible.

**Solutions:**
1. Press `Insert` key — the menu may be hidden, not absent
2. Check your keybind config: `toggle_menu=INSERT` must match your keyboard layout
3. Verify DirectX version is 11 or 12
4. Try switching from fullscreen to borderless windowed mode
5. Reinstall the Visual C++ Redistributable 2022

### 🟠 Activation Key Rejected

**Symptoms:** "Invalid Key" error at startup.

**Solutions:**
1. Ensure you're using the key exactly as generated — no extra spaces

4. Try: `.\StandLoader.exe --clear-auth` then re-enter your key

### 🔵 Custom DLC Packs Not Loading

**Symptoms:** Packs show in the list but don't apply in-game.

### 🟣 Cayo Perico Automation Stuck
